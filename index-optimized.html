Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How does ATS optimization work?

We analyze job descriptions for keywords and ensure your resume matches them exactly. We also optimize formatting for ATS parsing, improve keyword density, and ensure proper section headers that ATS systems recognize.

What makes tech resumes different?

Tech resumes require specific keywords, project-based formatting, quantifiable technical achievements, and industry-specific terminology that generic services often miss. We specialize exclusively in tech roles including software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, DevOps, and cloud architecture.

What's your turnaround time?

24 hours maximum for Basic plan, 12 hours for Pro plan, and 6 hours for Premium plan. Most resumes are processed even faster. Compare this to professional services that take 7+ days.

Do you offer revisions?

For the $10 Basic plan, we deliver one optimized version. The Pro plan includes 1 revision, and the Premium plan includes unlimited revisions within 30 days.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 100% money-back guarantee if you don't get at least 2x more interview calls within 30 days of using our optimized resume.

How do you ensure privacy?

We use encrypted file transfers, never share your data with third parties, and delete all files 30 days after delivery. Your resume is only seen by our AI system and human reviewers who sign strict NDAs.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, DOC, DOCX, and TXT files. We deliver optimized resumes in PDF and DOCX formats, along with a change summary document.

Can you help with specific tech roles?

Absolutely! We specialize in software engineering (frontend, backend, full-stack), data science, machine learning, cybersecurity, DevOps, cloud architecture, product management, and UX/UI design roles.
